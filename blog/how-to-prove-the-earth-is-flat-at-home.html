by Ian Thompson

You're also going to need some water. Now, we need to prepare to get wet. So, put on your bathing suit, your goggles, and get some towels ready!

First, get the rounded object. Then, pour the water on the rounded object. For the purposes of this article, the rounded object will be a lemon. Notice how the water just rolls of the lemon. Yes, a little bit of the water sticks on the the lemon, but a vast majority of it rolls right off. Now, get the plate and do the same thing with the plate lying flat. What do you notice?

Yep. Thats right. The water stay contained on the plate. How does this prove the earth is flat? It is really simple.
